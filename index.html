Rules
  • Board: 3×3 grid of mini tic‑tac‑toe boards (total 81 cells).
  • Turns: Players may play in any mini‑board on their turn.
  • Mini‑board win: First to 3 in a row (row/column/diagonal) captures that mini‑board. Captured boards are locked.
  • Mini‑board draw: If full with no winner, it is locked as a draw (doesn’t help either player for the big win).
  • Game win: First to capture 3 mini‑boards in a row on the big grid wins the match.
